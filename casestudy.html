Wire-framing Depop

Overview

Wire-framing Depop

This is taken from Katalina’s medium article about the UX/UI Prework at Ironhack which was to choose a mobile application we appreciate and deconstruct it down to its wireframe. Additionally, to then turn the wireframe into an interactive prototype.

About

In my personal life, I find myself most using second hand selling apps because it aligns with my values to shop and sell sustainably. Therefore, I decided to choose Depop, “a global marketplace — a mobile space where you can see what your friends and the people you’re inspired by are liking, buying, and selling”. A beautiful and simplistic idea, to which I was soon suprised to discover the intricacies that go into creating it.

Considerations

In thinking about my wireframe, I had to decide what the user task I would have liked to achieve. I wondered what someone who has never used the app before would do. Therefore, I decided upon making my idea of how someone would explore this new application.

People's Goals

In my mind, this user interaction would go from the (1) Explore Page, (2)My DNA page, (3)Feed and from any of those pages they can navigate to (4) Listing page or to(6)Profile Page/User Page.
